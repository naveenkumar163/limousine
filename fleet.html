Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Yes, pearsongtalimo is one of the top companies providing professional airport transportation services to and from Toronto’s major airports – Toronto Pearson International (YYZ) and Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (YTZ). They offer late-model luxury vehicles like sedans, SUVs and limousines chauffeured by professional drivers. pearsongtalimo allows convenient online booking and quoting for efficient airport transfers or hourly as-directed service.
Both Toronto Pearson and Billy Bishop Airport have shuttle bus services, though many travelers prefer the comfort and convenience of a private car service like pearsongtalimo. Their vehicles eliminate the need to navigate crowded shuttles with luggage. pearsongtalimo chauffeurs will be waiting to greet you at arrival doors.
Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport (YTZ) is located right in Toronto, on the Toronto Islands just a short 121-meter ferry crossing from downtown. Despite its convenient location, many travelers still opt for the luxury and hassle-free transportation provided by companies like pearsongtalimo to easily get to/from this regional airport.
The primary airport code for Toronto is YYZ, for Toronto Pearson International Airport. The smaller Billy Bishop Airport uses YTZ. When booking travel to Toronto, use the airport code YYZ unless your itinerary specifically routes through Billy Bishop (YTZ). Many travelers book pearsongtalimo for transportation either airport.
